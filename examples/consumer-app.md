# Example: Solana Consumer App

## Founder Prompt

I want to build a Solana-based social prediction app for crypto users.

---

## Founder OS Analysis

### Stage

Discover

---

### Problem Validation

Questions:

* Do users actively make predictions today?
* Where do they currently share predictions?
* What frustrations exist?

Assessment:

Potentially strong if prediction creation, sharing, and tracking are significantly better than existing workflows.

---

### ICP

Primary Users:

* Active crypto traders
* Prediction market participants
* Crypto-native social users

---

### MVP

Core Features:

* Create prediction
* Share prediction
* Track prediction outcomes

Exclude:

* Token
* Leaderboards
* Complex social features

---

### Launch Strategy

Pre-launch:

* Recruit 50 beta users
* Collect prediction examples
* Create educational content

Launch:

* Invite-only onboarding
* Community competitions

---

### Risks

* Weak retention
* Existing alternatives
* Low engagement loops

---

### Recommendation

Proceed with MVP validation before introducing incentives or tokens.
