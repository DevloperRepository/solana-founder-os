# Example: Solana Marketplace

## Founder Prompt

I want to build a marketplace for digital collectibles.

---

## Founder OS Analysis

### Stage

Discover

---

### Customer Discovery

Questions:

* What do creators struggle with?
* What do collectors struggle with?
* Why existing marketplaces are insufficient?

---

### Positioning

Focus on a specific niche first.

Avoid broad marketplace strategies.

---

### MVP

Core Features:

* Listings
* Discovery
* Purchases

Exclude:

* Complex social layers
* Governance
* Rewards systems

---

### Launch Strategy

Phase 1:

Recruit creators.

Phase 2:

Recruit collectors.

Phase 3:

Scale marketplace liquidity.

---

### Risks

* Liquidity fragmentation
* Cold-start problem
* Competition

---

### Recommendation

Start with a narrow niche and expand after achieving liquidity.
