# Example: Solana Consumer Social App

## Founder Prompt

I want to build a social platform where crypto users can publicly track predictions and build reputation on Solana.

---

## Stage Assessment

Current Stage:

Discover

Primary Goal:

Validate whether users care about public prediction reputation.

---

## Key Risks

* Users may not care about on-chain reputation.
* Existing social platforms may already solve the problem.
* Retention may depend on speculation.

---

## Recommended MVP

Include:

* Create prediction
* Track outcomes
* Public profile

Exclude:

* Token
* Governance
* Rewards
* Complex social features

---

## Validation Plan

Interview:

* Traders
* Prediction market users
* Crypto content creators

Goal:

Determine whether reputation creates meaningful value.

---

## Recommendation

Validate demand before introducing incentives, points, or tokens.
