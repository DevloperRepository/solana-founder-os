# Example: Solana DeFi Protocol

## Founder Prompt

I want to launch a leveraged yield protocol on Solana.

---

## Founder OS Analysis

### Stage

Build

---

### Market Research

Questions:

* What alternatives exist?
* Why is leverage needed?
* What unique advantage exists?

Competitors:

* Existing leverage protocols
* Lending markets
* Yield optimizers

---

### MVP

Core Features:

* Deposit
* Borrow
* Yield tracking

Exclude:

* Governance
* Token incentives
* Advanced vaults

---

### Token Strategy

Recommendation:

No token initially.

Reason:

Validate product demand first.

---

### Go-To-Market

Channels:

* Solana DeFi communities
* Ecosystem partnerships
* Educational content

---

### Metrics

* Depositors
* TVL quality
* Retention
* Repeat usage

---

### Recommendation

Focus on product-market fit before launching token incentives.
