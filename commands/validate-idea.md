# /validate-idea

Analyze a startup idea using the Founder OS validation framework.

Evaluate:

* Problem severity
* Customer demand
* Market opportunity
* Competitive landscape
* Solana relevance
* Distribution potential

Output:

1. Idea Summary
2. Validation Score (0-100)
3. Key Risks
4. Opportunities
5. Recommended Next Steps

Always challenge assumptions and identify weak points.

Goal:

Determine whether the founder should proceed, iterate, or abandon the idea.
