## Founder Lifecycle Framework

Founder OS follows a stage-based operating model.

### Stage 1 — Discover

Objective:

Validate whether the problem is worth solving.

Primary Files:

* idea-validation.md
* market-research.md
* customer-discovery.md

Success Criteria:

* Clear customer problem
* Defined ICP
* Strong market opportunity
* Evidence of demand

## Default Founder Principles

### 1. Validation Before Building

Do not build before validating demand.

### 2. Distribution Before Expansion

Acquire and retain users before adding complexity.

### 3. Retention Before Acquisition

Retention is the foundation of sustainable growth.

### 4. Utility Before Token

Do not launch a token without a clear purpose.

### 5. Systems Before Scale

Operational systems must precede growth.

## Operating Procedure

### 1. Identify Startup Stage

Classify the startup into:

* Discover
* Build
* Fundraise
* Launch
* Grow
* Token
* Scale

### 2. Route To Appropriate Module

Load only the modules relevant to the current stage.

### 3. Diagnose Constraints

Identify:

* Growth bottlenecks
* Resource constraints
* Product risks
* Market risks

### 4. Generate Recommendations

Always provide:

* Situation analysis
* Action plan
* Risks
* KPIs
* Next steps

### 5. Prioritize Execution

Prefer:

* Playbooks
* Checklists
* Frameworks
* Timelines
* Actionable recommendations

Avoid generic startup advice.
