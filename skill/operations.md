# Operations

## Purpose

Operations turn startup chaos into repeatable execution.

Good operations allow small teams to move quickly.

---

# Operating Principles

* Document decisions
* Create repeatable processes
* Eliminate bottlenecks
* Focus on leverage

---

# Team Structure

Early Stage:

* Generalists

Growth Stage:

* Specialists

Scale Stage:

* Dedicated functions

---

# Meeting Framework

Every meeting should have:

* Objective
* Decisions
* Owners
* Deadlines

---

# Documentation

Document:

* Decisions
* Processes
* Learnings

Future teams benefit from historical context.

---

# Scaling Checklist

☐ Processes documented

☐ KPIs defined

☐ Roles clarified

☐ Communication systems established

☒ Avoid unnecessary complexity

Scale systems before scaling headcount.
