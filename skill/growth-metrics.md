# Growth Metrics

## Purpose

Metrics exist to improve decisions.

Not to create dashboards.

---

# Core Growth Metrics

## Acquisition

* New users
* Signups
* Wallet connections

---

## Activation

* First meaningful action
* Time to value

---

## Retention

* Day 1
* Day 7
* Day 30

Retention is often the most important metric.

---

## Revenue

* Revenue
* Fees
* Customer value

---

# North Star Metric

Choose one metric that best represents value creation.

Examples:

* Active traders
* Active creators
* Active communities

---

# Diagnostic Framework

If growth slows:

Check:

1. Acquisition
2. Activation
3. Retention
4. Revenue

Identify the bottleneck before acting.

---

# Metrics Checklist

☐ North Star Metric defined

☐ Retention tracked

☐ Acquisition tracked

☐ Revenue tracked

☐ Growth bottlenecks identified

Measure what matters.
