# Launch Strategy

## Purpose

A launch is not an event.

A launch is the process of creating awareness, acquiring users, and establishing momentum.

Most launches fail because founders spend months building and only days planning distribution.

---

# Launch Objectives

Before launching define:

* Who is the target audience?
* What action should users take?
* What metric defines success?

---

# Launch Timeline

## Pre-Launch

30-90 Days Before

Goals:

* Build awareness
* Collect feedback
* Build relationships
* Grow distribution channels

Activities:

* Waitlists
* Beta users
* Community building
* Educational content
* Partnerships

---

## Launch Week

Goals:

* Maximize visibility
* Drive activation
* Capture feedback

Activities:

* Announcement threads
* Ecosystem amplification
* Community events
* Product demos
* Partner support

---

## Post Launch

Goals:

* Improve retention
* Fix onboarding issues
* Learn quickly

Activities:

* User interviews
* Product iteration
* KPI reviews

---

# Launch Metrics

Track:

* Signups
* Activated users
* Retention
* Referral rate
* Community growth

Avoid focusing only on impressions.

---

# Launch Checklist

☐ Distribution plan
