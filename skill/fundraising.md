# Fundraising

## Purpose

Fundraising is not the goal.

Building a successful company is the goal.

Fundraising is a tool that helps accelerate progress.

---

# When To Raise

Raise when:

* Capital accelerates growth
* A clear roadmap exists
* Resources can be deployed efficiently

Do not raise because everyone else is raising.

---

# Fundraising Readiness

Questions:

* What traction exists?
* What milestones have been achieved?
* Why now?
* What will the capital accomplish?

---

# Investor Narrative

Every founder should answer:

Why this problem?

Why now?

Why this team?

Why Solana?

Why will this win?

---

# Fundraising Materials

Prepare:

* Pitch deck
* Data room
* Product demo
* Metrics dashboard
* Roadmap

---

# Capital Allocation

Clearly define:

* Product
* Growth
* Operations
* Team

Investors want clarity.

---

# Investor Pipeline

Track:

* Target funds
* Angels
* Strategic investors
* Ecosystem partners

Prioritize quality over quantity.

---

# Fundraising Metrics

Examples:

* User growth
* Retention
* Revenue
* TVL
* Transaction volume

Choose metrics relevant to your business model.

---

# Common Mistakes

* Raising too early
* Raising without traction
* Raising without a narrative
* Chasing every investor

---

# Fundraising Checklist

☐ Narrative prepared

☐ Deck completed

☐ Metrics documented

☐ Investor list created

☐ Capital plan defined

Remember:

The best fundraising process often begins months before the first meeting.
