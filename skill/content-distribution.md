# Content Distribution

## Purpose

Content is distribution.

Distribution creates awareness.

Awareness creates opportunities.

Most startups publish content.

Few build content systems.

---

# Content Pillars

## Educational

Teach users.

Examples:

* Tutorials
* Guides
* Research

---

## Product

Explain:

* Features
* Updates
* Improvements

---

## Founder

Share:

* Lessons
* Decisions
* Challenges
* Insights

---

## Community

Highlight:

* Users
* Contributors
* Success stories

---

# Distribution Framework

Create Once

↓

Repurpose

↓

Distribute Across Channels

Examples:

Research
→ Thread
→ Telegram Post
→ Blog
→ Video

---

# Content Metrics

Track:

* Reach
* Engagement
* Click-through rate
* Conversion
* Community growth

Focus on business impact.

Not vanity engagement.
