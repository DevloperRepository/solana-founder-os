# Go-To-Market

## Purpose

Go-To-Market answers one question:

How will users discover and adopt the product?

Building is optional.

Distribution is mandatory.

---

# GTM Framework

Users move through five stages:

Awareness
↓
Interest
↓
Activation
↓
Retention
↓
Advocacy

Every growth strategy should support this funnel.

# Field Notes

## Distribution Is The Real Product

Many founders spend 90% of their time building and 10% thinking about distribution.

The market rewards the opposite.

---

## Partnerships Beat Advertising

In crypto, partnerships often outperform paid acquisition.

Trust transfers faster through ecosystems than advertisements.

---

## Community Before Token

A token cannot create a community.

A token can amplify an existing community.

Build people first.

Launch incentives later.

---

## Attention Is Temporary

Retention determines whether attention becomes growth.

Many launches succeed for one week.

Few succeed for one year.
