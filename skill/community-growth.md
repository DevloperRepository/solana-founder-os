# Community Growth

## Purpose

Strong communities create durable advantages.

The goal is not to maximize member count.

The goal is to create a group of users who repeatedly create value for each other and the product.

---

# Community Flywheel

Users
↓
Contributors
↓
Advocates
↓
Leaders
↓
Community Growth

---

# Community Stages

## Early Stage

Focus:

* Conversations
* Feedback
* User interviews
* Relationship building

Avoid:

* Over-moderation
* Complex structures

---

## Growth Stage

Focus:

* Ambassador programs
* Community events
* Educational initiatives
* User-generated content

---

## Mature Stage

Focus:

* Contributor programs
* Governance
* Leadership development

---

# Community Metrics

Track:

* Active members
* Returning members
* Contributor count
* Community-generated content
* Referral activity

Avoid vanity member counts.

---

# Ambassador Programs

Reward:

* Education
* Support
* Referrals
* Community leadership

Avoid rewarding spam activity.

---

# Community Checklist

☐ Clear purpose

☐ Community guidelines

☐ Feedback loops

☐ Contributor pathways

☐ Retention tracking
