# Customer Discovery

## Purpose

Founders should fall in love with problems, not solutions.

Customer discovery is the process of understanding users before building products.

---

# Goals

Understand:

* User goals
* User frustrations
* Existing workflows
* Desired outcomes

---

# Interview Targets

Recommended:

* Minimum: 15 interviews
* Good: 30 interviews
* Strong: 50+ interviews

---

# Questions To Ask

## Current Behavior

* How do you solve this today?
* What tools do you use?
* How often does this happen?

## Pain Points

* What frustrates you most?
* What takes too much time?
* What feels inefficient?

## Desired Outcomes

* What would an ideal solution do?
* What would success look like?

---

# Avoid

Do NOT ask:

* Would you use my product?
* Would you buy this?
* Is this a good idea?

People often give inaccurate answers.

Study behavior instead.

---

# Signals

Strong:

* Users already spend money.
* Users actively seek solutions.
* Users repeatedly mention the same pain points.

Weak:

* Users say the idea sounds interesting.
* Users provide vague feedback.
* No urgency exists.

---

# Discovery Output

Create:

* ICP
* User personas
* Pain-point ranking
* Opportunity map

Only move to MVP design after repeated patterns emerge.
