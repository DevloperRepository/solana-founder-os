# Partnerships

## Purpose

Partnerships accelerate growth through leverage.

The best partnerships create value for both sides.

---

# Types of Partnerships

## Distribution

Gain access to new audiences.

## Product

Integrations and technical collaboration.

## Strategic

Long-term ecosystem relationships.

## Community

Shared events and initiatives.

---

# Partner Selection

Evaluate:

* Audience overlap
* Strategic alignment
* Reputation
* Distribution capability

---

# Outreach Framework

1. Research

2. Identify alignment

3. Propose value

4. Execute quickly

5. Measure outcomes

---

# Partnership Metrics

Track:

* New users
* Engagement
* Revenue
* Referrals
* Retention

---

# Common Mistakes

* Chasing logos
* One-sided relationships
* Lack of follow-up

Partnerships are built on mutual value.
